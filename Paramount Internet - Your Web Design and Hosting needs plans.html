Informational e-commerce
This means you would like your website to contain information about your business, not selling anything online.

The most basic Informational websites usually contain about 5 or 6 pages, including options like HOME, ABOUT US, CONTACT US, etc.

More advanced informational websites may contain a list of thousands of products available, sometimes larger than e-commerce websites, but there is no option of buying online. You will need to contact the company to purchase anything.

Many informational websites have nothing to do with products. They may list detailed information about the company, and perhaps have a user login to access information.

 e-commerce websites define websites that include the ability to purchase. A website may have between ten to thousands of products. The e-commerce websites normally accept credit cards or other simular payment. Paypal is the most popular method of payment online and recommended because it accepts both credit card and Paypal customers (we can help you set it up).

e-commerce websites on an average have more time put in them and normally are more expensive than informational websites, but the easy purchasing for customers to buy "quickly" online make revenue so much easier for a business. Basically, it is worth the price many times over and an excellent investment. We also have procedures for advertising and customers finding you in search engines.
